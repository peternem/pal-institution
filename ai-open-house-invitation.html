Join us for the opening of the new Allen Institute building, a six-story, 270,000-square-foot life sciences building located in the bioscience hub of Seattle’s South Lake Union neighborhood. The program will be followed by a celebration at Seattle’s Museum of History and Industry, located just across from the Institute.

We’ll host a two-day Showcase Symposium prior to our Open House and Celebration. Showcase focuses on innovative approaches to big science, featuring presentations, team talks, lightening talks and poster sessions from Allen Institute scientists, as well as presentations from this year’s Next Generation Leaders.
